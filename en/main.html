Welcome to Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel

Nestled on the pristine shores of Chorvoq Lake, approximately 60 kilometers northeast of Tashkent, Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel represents the perfect fusion of luxury, comfort, and natural beauty. Our hotel stands as a beacon of hospitality in one of Uzbekistan's most breathtaking locations.

Since opening our doors, we have been committed to providing our guests with an exceptional experience that combines world-class amenities, impeccable service, and the stunning natural surroundings of Chorvoq Lake. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our hotel offers the perfect retreat from the bustling capital while remaining conveniently accessible.

Our Story

Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel was conceived with a vision to create a destination that would showcase the natural beauty of the Chorvoq region while providing guests with the highest standards of international hospitality. As part of the globally recognized Wyndham Hotels & Resorts family, we bring decades of hospitality expertise to this unique location.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our hotel, from the carefully designed rooms and suites to the world-class dining experiences and recreational facilities. We take pride in being more than just a hotel – we are a gateway to experiencing the best of Uzbekistan's natural wonders and hospitality traditions.

Our Philosophy

At Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel, we believe that true luxury lies in the perfect balance of comfort, service, and authentic experiences. Our philosophy is built on four pillars:

  • Guest-Centric Service: Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to exceeding guest expectations
  • Environmental Stewardship: We are dedicated to preserving the natural beauty that surrounds us through sustainable practices
  • Cultural Authenticity: We celebrate and share the rich cultural heritage of Uzbekistan with our guests
  • Continuous Innovation: We constantly evolve our services and facilities to meet the changing needs of modern travelers

Our Facilities

Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel offers an extensive array of world-class facilities designed to cater to every guest's needs:

  • Elegantly appointed rooms and suites with lake or mountain views
  • Multiple dining venues featuring international and local cuisine
  • Olympic-sized outdoor swimming pool with panoramic lake views
  • Full-service spa and wellness center
  • Modern fitness center equipped with state-of-the-art equipment
  • Private beach area with water sports facilities
  • Professional conference and event spaces
  • Tennis courts and recreational areas
  • 24-hour concierge and guest services

The Chorvoq Experience

Chorvoq Lake, often called the "Tashkent Sea," is a stunning man-made reservoir surrounded by the majestic Tian Shan mountains. The lake and its surrounding areas offer year-round attractions, from water sports in summer to winter activities in the nearby mountains. Our hotel serves as the perfect base for exploring this remarkable region.

Guests can enjoy a wide range of activities including swimming, boating, fishing, hiking, and simply relaxing while taking in the breathtaking mountain and lake views. In winter, the nearby ski resorts become accessible, making our hotel an ideal destination for winter sports enthusiasts.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We recognize our responsibility to protect the pristine environment that makes Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel so special. Our sustainability initiatives include:

  • Energy-efficient systems throughout the property
  • Water conservation and recycling programs
  • Waste reduction and recycling initiatives
  • Use of locally sourced products and ingredients
  • Support for local communities and conservation efforts
  • Environmental education programs for guests and staff

Awards & Recognition

Our commitment to excellence has been recognized through various awards and accolades, including recognition for our service standards, facilities, and contribution to tourism in Uzbekistan. We are proud to be part of the Wyndham Hotels & Resorts family, one of the world's largest and most diverse hotel companies.

Location & Accessibility

While offering a peaceful retreat from urban life, our hotel remains easily accessible:

  • 60 kilometers from Tashkent city center (approximately 50 minutes by car)
  • 70 kilometers from Tashkent International Airport (approximately 1 hour)
  • Close to major highways with convenient transportation options
  • Private transfer services available upon request
  • Ample parking facilities for guests arriving by car

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow and evolve, our commitment to providing exceptional hospitality experiences remains unwavering. We constantly seek new ways to enhance our facilities, expand our services, and exceed the expectations of our valued guests. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, a corporate retreat, or a special event, Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel offers the perfect setting for creating unforgettable memories.

We invite you to experience the unique blend of natural beauty, modern luxury, and warm Uzbek hospitality that defines Wyndham Garden Chorvoq Hotel. Come discover why we are the premier destination on the shores of Chorvoq Lake.
